Multipurpose Double-Ended Travel Cutlery
Overview
A compact, reusable, and eco-friendly eating tool that combines two utensils (spoon–fork or spoon–knife) into a single unit. Designed for travelers, students, office workers, and outdoor users.
Problem
People rely on disposable plastic cutlery or carry multiple utensils while traveling, which is inconvenient and environmentally harmful.
Solution
A lightweight, double-ended cutlery design that:
Reduces space and weight
Improves hygiene
Supports sustainability
Is easy to clean and durable
Materials
Stainless Steel (AISI 304) – strong, corrosion-resistant
Food-grade Polypropylene (PP) – lightweight grip
Optional Bamboo – eco-friendly handle
Results
High functionality
Comfortable grip (85% positive feedback)
Hygienic and easy to clean (90% positive feedback)
Portable and durable
Conclusion
An affordable, practical, and sustainable solution for modern eating needs, suitable for travel and daily use.
